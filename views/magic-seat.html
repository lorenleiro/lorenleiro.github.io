Magic Seat

Flatus is a powerfull mage how likes crafting rare potions. Unfortunately, even the most powerfull mages need to face their destiny. Will you be able to help Flatus before something bad happens?

This game was created for the 1ยบ edition of Ghrelo Jam! in 6 days.

Me and a group of friends participated on the Ghrelo Jam! The theme of tha jam was Light, so we created Flatus: a mage capable of invoking light orbs that will help him find something important in the darkness of his house... Magic seat is a puzzle physics game, featuring numerous mechanics like light candles and moving objects, breaking potions to trigger it's effects. The main goal was finding the last toilet paper and trying to not tear everything appart. A short but very funny experience.

Main features

  • Physics based

  • Moving/trowing objects and potions to trigger their effects

  • Numerous potions with different outcomes

  • Toilet paper appears on random locations

  • Various lighting effects that affect gameplay

What was the challenge?

Creating a physics game and make everything feel what is supossed to feel was not an easy task. Finally I find a way to make each object interactable for the player with different properties, but without modifying it's gameplay. All the potions modifies the objects behaviour, so we have multiple unique ways to interact with the objects of the world. Also, the potions can undone bad things the player made, or make it worse, so it was a nice addition that feels great inside the game.

Project duration: 10/10/2022 - 10/16/2022
