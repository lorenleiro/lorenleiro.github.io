Magic Seat

Flatus is a powerful mage who likes to make rare potions. Unfortunately, even the most powerful mages must face a cruel fate.

This game was created for the 1ยบ edition of Ghrelo Jam! in 6 days.

Me and a group of friends participated in the Ghrelo Jam! The theme of the jam was light, so we created Flatus: a mage who can summon balls of light that will help him find something important in the darkness of his house... Magic Seat is a puzzle physics game that includes numerous mechanics such as light candles and moving objects to breaking potions to trigger their effects. The main objective is to find the last toilet paper and try not to tear everything to pieces. A short but very entertaining experience.

Main features

  • Physics based game

  • Moving/throwing objects and potions to trigger their effects

  • Different potions with specific effects

  • Toilet paper appears on random locations

  • Lighting directly affects gameplay

What was the challenge?

It was no easy task to create a physics game and make everything feel the way it's supposed to feel. I created a base object from which each object will inherit basic properties. From there, we can further tune the values to make each object feel different. All potions change the behavior of the objects, so we have several unique ways to interact with objects in the world. Other great additions where potions. Potions can undo mistakes made by the player or make the scenario worse, so the player must learn what each potion does and when to use them.

Project duration: 10/10/2022 - 16/12/2022
