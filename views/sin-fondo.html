Sin Fondo

Explore the depths of the ocean with your submarine while other creatures try to destroy you. Upgrade it to go deeper and deeper. What lies down there?

Sin Fondo offers a complete submarine upgrade system that allows the player to improve his submarine and make it more powerful. The main objective is to complete as many missions as possible to earn coins. Every part of the submarine can be upgraded: engine, fuel depot, torpedo launcher, hull... but each time you upgrade it, the game gets harder and harder. This keeps the player on their toes and keeps the game challenging. When the player finally manages to reach the bottom of the abyss, his skills and powers are put to the test by something more dangerous than anything else.

Main features

  • Submarine upgrade system

  • Ammo, fuel and other consumables

  • Difficulty level increases depending on submarine upgrades

  • Procedurally generated missions depending on game difficulty

  • Currency system

  • Sonar systems that help the player complete missions

  • Ambient effects and music make the game even more captivating

  • Different enemy variants with their strengths and weaknesses

  • Enemies get stronger the deeper you go

  • Final Boss Battle & End Game

What was the challenge?

I wanted the game to be as inmersive as possible. Due to my lack of artistic ability, the UI and the environment look bad, but I was able to make the game immersive by adding things that helped create a deep atmosphere: the sound, the music, the lighting and the effects, all of this was supported by a docking system that lets you upgrade and refill your submarine so the player can focus on progressing through the game.

Project duration: 26/02/2022 - 15/03/2022
