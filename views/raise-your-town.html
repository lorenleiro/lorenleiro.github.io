Raise your Town!

Help JM create a powerfull new Kingdom! Expand your little settlement and protect it from the dangers of the night.

Sin Fondo was my first "ambitious" project, it features a fully upgradeble system allowing the player to upgrade their submarine to be more powerfull. The main objetive here ir trying to upgrade your submarine as much as possible before going to the end of the Abyss, there the player will fight their last challenge.

Main features

  • Grid base building

  • Play as JM inside your base

  • Day/night cycle

  • Enemy waves

  • Fast forward time

  • In-game currency system

  • Upgrade your base and character weapons

  • Interact with buildings and NPCs

  • Ultimate-Form defenses at max level

  • Varieaty of enemies & bosses

What was the challenge?

This was my first time creating a game with a team. We subdivided task and I was encomended the main core mechanics and gameplay. One of the tougthes things I've made was the save/load system. We needed to save all the grid information and then recreating it so the player can cotinue the game. Other features such as the realtime UI, enemy AI and pathfinding, wave spawns, and core base building were also worked by me.

Project duration: 10/03/2022 - 10/04/2022
