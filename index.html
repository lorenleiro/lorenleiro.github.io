About me

Here you can read a little about me.

Hi. My name is Lorenzo Torres Rodríguez and I'm a game developer located in Spain. In 2021 I started my own path to learn how to develop video games. I took a new course about game development and virtual reality and after that I started playing and learning by developing my own games alone, and sometimes with people from all over the world.

I developed a passion for video games at a very early age. Since I left high school, my goal has been to get a job in this industry and learn how to become a good developer by using the best practices such as coding patterns. I'm eager to continue improving myself.
